History Of Premier

Once a humble inn, the hotel evolved over a century into an iconic destination, blending timeless charm with modern elegance. Renovations, renowned service, and a rich heritage define its journey, captivating guests worldwide. Founded in 1920, the hotel originally catered to weary travelers along a bustling trade route. Through wars and economic fluctuations, it endured, expanding and upgrading to host dignitaries, celebrities, and discerning travelers, becoming an emblem of hospitality excellence.
